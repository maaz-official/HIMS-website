OUR FACILITIES

WELL-FURNISHED CLASSROOMS

HIMS has provided students with well-furnished & air-conditioned classrooms which gives a better and healthier environment. All classrooms are spacious enough for students to sit in the classes comfortably.

The environment in the classrooms will influence and encourage the students to acquire knowledge, which will lead them to be better professionals. All classrooms are fully equipped with multimedia, projectors, desktops, desks, whiteboards, and a central sound system.

COMPUTER/IT LAB

HIMS computer lab is equipped with Core i3 computers along with central Internet (DSL Broad band/10mb/Xeon server) and all the latest software for students to learn basic computer skills, do their assignments easily and look up for any course related work.
