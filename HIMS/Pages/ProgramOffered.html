PROGRAMS OFFERED

BACHELORS IN NURSING (BSN) 4 YEARS

The BSN program is spread over 8 semesters and the total credit hours for overall program is 139, designed by Higher Education Commission (HEC). The program is recognized by the Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) and affiliated with Khyber Medical University (KMU), Peshawar.

Main focus of the program is to prepare knowledgeable, clinically competent professional nurses who will be able to provide comprehensive care at primary, secondary and tertiary levels within hospitals and community settings. The program imparts theoretical knowledge and skills which are grounded in evidence-based practice to ensure safe and high-quality nursing care. In addition to theoretical knowledge and skills, the program aims to enhance critical thinking, decision making and communication skills.

POST REGISTERED NURSE (POST RN BSN) 2 YEARS

The Post RN BSN program is spread over 4 semesters and total credit hours of curriculum designed and recognized by Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) for overall program is 60. The program affiliated with Khyber Medical University (KMU) Peshawar.

The Post-RN BSN program is a two years program designed for experienced registered nurses. This program provides an opportunity to acquire in-depth knowledge and skills needed for clinically competent professional nurses.

DIPLOMA OF LADY HEALTH VISITOR

HIMS CON is pleased to offer the Diploma of Lady Health Visitor (LHV), a comprehensive two-year diploma program that is recognized by both the Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) and the Nursing Examination Board (NEB) in Peshawar. Our LHV diploma program is designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills necessary to become a compassionate and competent Lady Health Visitor.

Students will receive training in a variety of areas, including maternal and child health, family planning, nutrition, and disease prevention. Through a combination of classroom learning and practical experience, our students will develop the expertise and confidence needed to serve their communities effectively. Upon graduation, our LHV diploma graduates will be equipped to make a positive impact in the health sector and pursue a fulfilling career in healthcare as Lady Health Visitors.
